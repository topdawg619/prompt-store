## Email 2 — Customization Tips
**Send:** Day 3 after purchase  
**Subject line options:**
- "The 3-minute trick to make every prompt sound like YOU"
- "How to customize your prompts for your exact niche"
- "Make these prompts 10x more powerful (takes 3 minutes)"

---

**Body:**

Hey [First Name],

Hope you've had a chance to try a few prompts from your pack. 

If you've already used them — awesome, you know what I mean when I say the output quality is different.

If you haven't gotten there yet — no worries, that's actually why I'm writing today.

I want to show you the fastest way to make every prompt in your pack feel 100% personalized to your business.

**The Prompt Customization Formula:**

Every prompt in your pack has placeholder brackets like [your niche] or [target audience]. Here's how to fill those in fast:

**Step 1 — Write your "business card paragraph" once:**
> *"I am a [role] who helps [target customer] achieve [outcome]. My style is [tone]. My business is called [name] and I operate in [location/market]."*

Save this somewhere easy to access (Notes app, sticky note, doc).

**Step 2 — Paste it before any prompt:**
> *"Using this context: [paste your paragraph] — now do the following: [paste the prompt]"*

**Step 3 — Iterate fast:**
If the output isn't quite right, don't start over. Just say:
> *"Make it [shorter / more casual / more specific to X / less salesy]"*

ChatGPT will refine without losing the structure.

**Bonus tip — build a "voice sample" library:**

Grab 3–5 pieces of your best past writing (emails, posts, descriptions). Paste them in and say:
> *"This is a sample of my writing voice. Match this tone for everything going forward in this conversation."*

The output will sound like you wrote it.

These techniques work across every prompt in the pack — and honestly, across everything you do with ChatGPT going forward.

Let me know which prompts have been most useful so far — always curious what's clicking for people.

— [Your Name]

*P.S. Replying to this email is the easiest way to reach me. I answer personally.*

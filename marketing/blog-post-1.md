## 1. Listing Description Prompts

**The problem:** Writing compelling listing copy for every property is exhausting.

**The prompt formula:**
> "Write a compelling MLS listing description for a [bedrooms]-bed, [bathrooms]-bath [property type] in [neighborhood], [city]. Key features include [list 5–7 features]. The target buyer is [buyer persona]. Tone: [warm/luxury/modern]. Max 250 words. Highlight lifestyle over specs."

**Why it works:** Specifying the buyer persona forces the AI to frame features as *benefits*. "Granite countertops" becomes "a kitchen designed for Sunday morning pancakes with the family."

**Pro variation:** Add "Include a soft call-to-action at the end encouraging a private showing" and watch your showing requests climb.

---

## 2. Lead Nurture Email Prompts

**The problem:** Following up with cold leads feels awkward and time-consuming.

**The prompt formula:**
> "Write a friendly, non-pushy follow-up email to a lead who toured a home 2 weeks ago but hasn't responded. Acknowledge they may still be deciding. Offer value by including 2–3 market insights about [neighborhood]. End with a low-pressure CTA to schedule a quick call. Keep it under 150 words. Tone: warm and professional."

**Why it works:** The "low-pressure" instruction is key. ChatGPT defaults to aggressive CTAs. This prompt produces emails that feel human — because the AI is explicitly told to be helpful, not salesy.

---

## 3. Market Report Prompts

**The problem:** Monthly market reports take hours to write. Most agents skip them.

**The prompt formula:**
> "Write a 200-word monthly market update for [city/neighborhood] homeowners. Include these stats: [median price], [days on market], [list-to-sale ratio]. Frame the data as good news for sellers OR buyers (choose based on data). Tone: authoritative but approachable. End with a question that invites replies."

**Why it works:** Ending with a question drives engagement. Replies open conversations. Conversations close deals.

---

## 4. Social Media Caption Prompts

**The problem:** Posting consistently on Instagram and Facebook is a grind.

**The prompt formula:**
> "Write 5 Instagram captions for a real estate agent in [city]. Mix content types: 1 educational, 1 listing highlight, 1 client win (keep it vague), 1 behind-the-scenes, 1 market tip. Each caption under 150 words. Include 5 relevant hashtags per post. Tone: [choose: approachable/luxury/energetic]."

**Why it works:** Batch-generating 5 captions at once means you can schedule a week of content in 10 minutes.

---

## 5. Objection-Handling Scripts

**The problem:** Every agent hears the same objections. Few have polished responses ready.

**The prompt formula:**
> "Write 3 conversational responses to the seller objection: '[objection text]'. Each response should acknowledge the concern, reframe it positively, and pivot to a next step. Keep each response under 100 words. Tone: calm, confident, consultative."

**Common objections to plug in:**
- "The market is too slow right now."
- "I want to wait until spring."
- "Your commission is too high."
